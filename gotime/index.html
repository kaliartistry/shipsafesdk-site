Excursion Timing Intelligence

Meeting-point navigation inside the cruise app.

GoTime uses excursion booking context, meeting-point coordinates, walking ETA, and alert priority rules to support shore excursion teams without creating a second passenger experience.

Meeting-point walking ETA

Passengers see walking time to the relevant meeting point, not just a map pin or straight-line distance.

Excursion departure alerts

Alerts escalate as the margin to the excursion meet-by time narrows, using thresholds configured for the partner pilot.

Return-to-ship priority

When return-to-ship risk conflicts with an excursion alert, ShipSafe prioritizes the ship return window.
